We're Here to Serve You Well

Your Strength. Our Vision.

Inspired by Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, Aura Centers empower women through fitness by offering premium facilities, expert guidance, & a supportive environment. As one of Jeddah's first luxury gyms for women, we promote a healthy lifestyle and positive social space. Our two branches – Aura Luxury (North Obhur) and Aura Elite (Corniche) – provide more than just fitness; they're a second home for our members.

OUR CLASSES

FITNESS CLASSES FOR EVERY GOAL

OUR MISSION

To help women understand that physical fitness is crucial for healthy mind and body. To provide a comfortable and pleasant environment for women to continue their healthy and fitness journey

OUR VISION

To inspire and empower women in Saudi Arabia to lead healthier, happier lives by providing world-class fitness experiences in an environment of luxury, support, and community—contributing to the goals of Vision 2030.

AURA ELITE

We at Aura Junior strongly believe that physical activity for kids is essential for their health and emotional well-being. For this reason, we offer a variety of programs for children in our well-equipped and hygienic kids’ fitness center. Located in the heart of Jeddah, at Corniche and North Obhur, Aura Junior has unique packages specially designed with the health and fitness needs of our young stars in mind. Our dedicated staff is professionally qualified to provide fitness training in a safe environment.

AURA LUXURY

We bring to you a luxurious fitness center right in your neighborhood. Aura ladies’ gym is not just another next-door gym. We have highly professional & qualified trainers, huge premises, spacious studios, a ravishing swimming pool, well maintained jacuzzis, a full-service spa, and a stylish café that serves healthy food and Starbucks. We offer a variety of unique workout programs at our wellness club. Are you ready to start your fitness journey in a luxurious environment?
